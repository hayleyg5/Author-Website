ROBYN RACHKOWSKI

Hello. It’s so nice to meet you.
I’m Robyn. Like most authors, I’ve always struggled with daydreaming too deeply into the stories in my own head. I love to write, whether it be for my university lab reports and project deliverables or as a former editor of an engineering student-run newspaper.
I started writing fiction again every night (and sometimes multiple times a day) in 2022. My debut novel Two Kingdoms, that I am currently querying, was the result of these many late nights. I am so happy to finally begin to share the story and characters that have been in my head for almost a decade.
I grew up in Northern Ontario, Canada, and studied chemical engineering at uOttawa. When I’m not helping consolidate and repatriate used nuclear fuel at Canadian Nuclear Laboratories, you can find me on my yoga mat in a half-pigeon pose, on the back of a 16.3hh bay gelding named Darwin, or drinking tea while shuffling my tarot cards.
